At Tristar Labs, we prioritize your privacy. The OmniVids extension only processes the public URLs you submit for download. We do not track your general browsing history, nor do we store your personal social media passwords.

Any account information collected during the purchase process is strictly used for:

We use industry-standard, secure payment gateways (such as Paddle) to process transactions. Your credit card data never touches our servers directly.
